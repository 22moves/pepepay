Pepe Pay (PPP) Emission Model

Pepe Pay (PPP) employs a deflationary mechanism in its token emission model, resulting in a gradual reduction of the total token supply over time. This deflation is achieved through a process known as "token burning. This unique approach encourages long-term holding and has the potential to drive the value of PPP tokens higher as their scarcity increases.

Unlike traditional centralized systems, Pepe Pay's emission model relies on a decentralized network and consensus mechanism to validate and distribute new tokens. This approach ensures that no single entity has control over the token issuance process, promoting transparency and trust within the Pepe Pay community.
